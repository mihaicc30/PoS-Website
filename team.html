HIPPOS Project
HIPPOS Team was founded by a group of students, started of as a challenge and them pushing it to the limits to explore all the possibilities while applying what was studied. It is all about the passion for programming and making life easier through automation. On the plus side to make some profit to buy coffees, that is like a dream come true.
